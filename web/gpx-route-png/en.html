Track traversal animation

The route draws from start to finish: the full line and all points start white, then your chosen color travels along the path. Labels and points recolor when the animation reaches them. Uses the same settings as step 1.

Upload a GPX in step 1 to preview the traversal animation here.
3

Transparent track PNG

Same geometry as step 1: a track layer on a transparent background for photo or video overlays.

Upload a GPX in step 1 and the track will appear over the photo here.
